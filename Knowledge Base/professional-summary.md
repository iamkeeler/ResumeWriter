Gary Keeler is a strategic product design leader with over 14 years of experience driving end-to-end product development, from conception through launch, in the healthcare, enterprise, and consumer sectors. He has a proven track record of scaling design teams by 300% while delivering significant business impact, including generating a $55M pipeline and achieving a 1400% improvement in conversion rates. Gary is expert at integrating user needs with business objectives and technical constraints to build products that achieve product-market fit and revenue growth.

His recent role as Senior Director of Marketing, Brand, and Creative at Veranex Solutions involved leading enterprise-scale product strategy and marketing growth, digital transformation initiatives, and architecting multiple iterations of website redesigns that resulted in substantial improvements in lead generation, conversion rates, and web traffic. He also implemented an enterprise-wide CRM rollout, unlocking personalized marketing segmentation and customer journey orchestration that increased qualified lead conversion by 25% year over year.

Previously, as Director of User Experience at Veranex, Gary built and scaled the UX function from scratch, establishing rigorous user research practices, design systems, and design operations. He successfully scaled the design team, mentored emerging leaders, and drove user research initiatives that increased project continuation rates by 50%, with all projects delivering on time and without scope failures.

At Zebra Technologies, Gary directed a $18M custom software product portfolio, defined product strategy, pricing, packaging and bundling, and go-to-market for over 50 products, and led a design practice delivering transformational technology solutions to global enterprise clients including Amazon, Walmart, Disney, and USPS. He established methodologies and service delivery models adopted across multiple regions, built high-performing teams, and contributed to a 33% revenue increase through new digital consulting offerings.

Gary’s skills include product strategy, UX research, design systems, leadership in team scaling and mentorship, Agile methodologies, technical collaboration, product analytics, and marketing automation. He has also contributed to open source tools for UX prototyping, delivered conference presentations on design leadership, and holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design from the University of Wisconsin - Stout.

This comprehensive background highlights Gary’s ability to drive innovation, build scalable design organizations, and deliver measurable business outcomes through user-centered design and strategic marketing leadership.
