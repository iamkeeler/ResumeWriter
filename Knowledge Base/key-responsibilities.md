## Key Responsibilities

### Senior Director of Marketing, Brand, and Creative | *Veranex*
*(March 2023 - Present)*
*   **Scope & Team:** Unified and managed the creative and marketing technology functions.
    *   **Team Structure:** Directly managed 1 internal creative lead and 4 external agency partners. Collaborated within a wider marketing org of ~10 people (including Product Marketing Leads, Content Leads, and SVP).
    *   **Agency & Vendor Management:** Owned relationships and performance for 6 distinct agency partners covering Google Analytics, Google Ads, Website Development, HubSpot/Salesforce operations, and conference vendors.
    *   **Conference Operations:** Owned and operated all conference contracting and execution, serving as the primary decision-maker for event strategy and logistics.
*   **Financials:** Managed a budget of ~$600k (approx. 60% of the total marketing budget), with direct P&L responsibility for creative operations and conference/event spend.
*   **Key Outcome:** Consolidated fragmented operations into a cohesive unit, aligning creative execution with broader marketing strategy.

### Interim Head of Marketing | *Veranex*
*(July 2024 - Dec 2024)*
*   **Role Type:** Crisis management and operational turnaround.
*   **Scope:** Unified two previously siloed marketing teams into a singular, performance-driven organization of 10 people (Graphic Designer, Copywriter, 3 Marketing Leads, PM, Internal Comms, + Agency Contractors).
*   **Operational Overhaul:**
    *   **Workflow:** Shifted from ad-hoc/waterfall to Agile-inspired daily standups for focused alignment.
    *   **Management:** Instituted weekly one-on-ones to drive accountability and regular executive reporting to demonstrate turnaround progress.
    *   **Strategy:** Cut non-value-add activities to focus resources exclusively on high-impact initiatives.
*   **Outcome:** Rebuilt the team's operating model and drove a **$55M pipeline** through the restructured approach.

### Director of User Experience | *Veranex*
*(Nov 2021 - March 2023)*
*   **Scope:** Built and scaled the UX function for the healthcare services division.
*   **Growth:** Scaled team by **300%**, recruiting and onboarding 6 UX designers.
*   **Utilization Management:** Responsible for maintaining a **70-80% billable utilization target** for the team while balancing non-billable initiatives like design culture building and governance frameworks.
*   **Player-Coach Role:** Operated with a **70/30 split** (70% management/strategy, 30% billable execution), personally contributing to client projects to maintain "sharpness" and lead by example.

### Acting Director, Software Practice | *Zebra Technologies*
*(Oct 2020 - Nov 2021)*
*   **Scope:** Directed an $18M custom software product portfolio.
*   **Delivery Management:** Oversaw a delivery organization of ~20 staff (Engineers, Designers, Project Managers) and external PM partners.
    *   **Project Volume:** Managed **10-15 concurrent active projects** at any given time.
*   **Operational Oversight:**
    *   **Management:** Weekly direct report one-on-ones and bi-weekly/monthly skip-level meetings.
    *   **Risk Management:** Utilized DevOps dashboards to track project health, intervening personally when projects showed "orange" risk indicators to ensure delivery stability.

### Design Practice Manager | *Zebra Technologies*
*(Aug 2015 - Nov 2020)*
*   **Scope:** Led the design practice for a $12M business line.
*   **Role Type:** Hybrid Player-Coach / Principal Designer.
    *   **Strategy:** Identified and "packaged" service offerings into standalone products (Go-to-Market strategy).
    *   **Execution:** Acted as the "Fixer" across the design team, stepping in to resolve complex blockers so designers could maintain velocity.
*   **Deliverable:** Responsible for both the business strategy (defining the offering) and the high-level design execution (principal design work).

### Director of Design | *ITR Mobility*
*(June 2011 - Aug 2015)*
*   **Scope:** Established the design function from scratch, growing the team from 1 to 5 designers.
*   **Foundational Role:** Started as an **80% Hands-On Player / 20% Coach**.
    *   **Evolution:** Progressed from Intern to Director. Initially split duties with a peer (Platform vs. Services), then built the Services design team via a structured internship-to-hire pipeline (Intern -> Junior -> Mid -> Senior).
*   **Philosophy:** Maintained hands-on involvement throughout tenure to ensure relevance as a mentor and leader.
