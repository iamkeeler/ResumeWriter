Job Description
Are you naturally curious? Do you see the world as it might be and wonder “what if”? Do you get excited about how technology and software can make things better? Are you already creating products with smart agentic AI-powered solutions? Would you like to work on a market-leading product that improves the movement of goods in the global economy? If so, keep reading.

The U.S. Bank Freight Payment Product Team is currently seeking a Senior Product Manager to own and oversee the strategic product management of a suite of agentic AI-powered products that support stronger supply chains and improved collaboration between large organizations and their transportation and shipping partners.

The incumbent will bring an “AI-first” mindset to their job, evaluating, designing, and developing agentic AI-powered products. They will also help champion an AI-first mindset across the Freight Payment team, and in doing so will work with a variety of business partners within and outside the bank to identify, define, and cultivate opportunities to meet customer needs.

The art of the possible is shifting rapidly in the application of AI-based solutions. The U.S. Bank Freight Payment Product Team seeks a self-starter, self-learner, internal bridge-builder and technology sponge interested in absorbing all that is happening transforming that information into insights and ideas for new agentic AI-powered product offerings, product positioning, product development and pricing philosophy. You’ll need the ability to ideate as well as execute.

If you’re not interested in how goods get transported around the world, don’t apply. If you’re not interested in collaborating with sales, operations, finance and technology partners, don’t apply. If you’re not ready to uncover a customer’s unmet needs by combining the voice of the customer with your strategic thinking skills, don’t apply.

Product Management experience
